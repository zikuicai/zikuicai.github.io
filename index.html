Hi, I am Zikui Cai

Currently a Ph.D. candidate in Electrical and Computer Engineering at UC Riverside, advised by Professor M. Salman Asif.
My research interests broadly span the area of computer vision, computational imaging, and (adversarial) machine learning. In the past, I was fortunate to have interned at UII America (Summer 2022 in Cambridge, MA), working closely with Ziyan Wu, Benjamin Planche, and Terrence Chen ; and at PARC (Summer 2021 in Palo Alto, CA) working with Shantanu Rane.
